Each card should have a Number for the prefix.
Followed by the Card Name.
The Suffix should be an abbreviation of the card type.
    _AC = Action Card
    _BC = Boss Card
    _CC = Character Card
    _CrC = Creature Card
    _EfC = Effect Card
    _EnC = Enemy Card
    _LC = Location Card
    _TC = Tech Card
    _WC = Weapon Card

CardNumber_CardName_CardType

Example: 1_Samus_CC
